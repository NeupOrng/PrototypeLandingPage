I live outside Cambodia. How do I take the entrance Exam?
You may take the exam anytime. Just register and take the entrance exam online.
*Make sure you have stable internet connection and a laptop with camera.

What kind of examination will I be taking?
The entrance exam consists of an IQ test and English Language Proficiency test.
*For Software Engineering applicants, a Mathtest will also be given. An Interview in English will be conducted forthose who pass the written exam.

How long is the exam?
Software Engineering: 3 hours
Tourism Management: 1 hour

How will I be interviewed and how long is the interview?
Entrance exam passers will be scheduled for an online interview via: google hangouts.
The interview may take 40-60 minutes and is conducted in English.

How and when do I get the results?
Exam results will be sent to you via email within 7 days.

How many times can I take the entrance exam?
An applicant can only take the exam once.

When can I enroll?
KIT accepts enrollment for April and October intakes.

How much is the tuition fee?
Please check our tuition fee breakdown at: campus.quipper.com

How do we pay tuition?
International money transfer (Indonesia to Cambodia)
Please send money to A2A town Cambodia bank account.

Do you offer scholarship?
We offer a Cashback Program for international students. You will initially pay for your tuition and living expenses. International KIT students who excel and meet KIT requirements for the program may get a FULL REFUND on tuition fee.
For more information, please visit: campus.quipper.com

Where will I stay while studying at KIT?
KIT provides accommodation, meals and laundry service as part of the boarding school system.

Do you have a campus tour?
Yes. Please inquire at: infokit@kit.edu.kh
